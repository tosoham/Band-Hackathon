# Encryption Policy

## At rest

Customer data is encrypted at rest using cloud-provider managed encryption primitives.

## In transit

Customer traffic is encrypted in transit using TLS.

## Key management

Standard deployments use SentinelAI-managed keys. Customer-managed key options require enterprise scoping.

## Approved wording

Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Customer-managed key options may be available for enterprise deployments after technical scoping.
