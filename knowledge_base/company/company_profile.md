# SentinelAI Security Platform

## Approved positioning

SentinelAI Security Platform provides AI-assisted security workflow automation for enterprise and public-sector teams. The platform helps security teams triage questionnaires, map evidence, and maintain auditable approval trails.

## Boundaries

SentinelAI does not replace Legal, Security, Privacy, or Compliance approval. High-risk commitments require human review before they are included in a final RFP response.

## Owner

Sales Engineering owns positioning. Legal and Security own final approved language.
