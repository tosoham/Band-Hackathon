# Data Residency

## EU hosting

Primary customer data can be hosted in the EU for eligible deployments.

## Operational telemetry

Limited operational telemetry may be processed globally for reliability, abuse prevention, support, and security monitoring.

## Approved wording

Primary customer data can be hosted in the EU. Limited operational telemetry may be processed globally under our DPA and subprocessors list.

## Forbidden wording

Do not say all data remains exclusively in the EU.
