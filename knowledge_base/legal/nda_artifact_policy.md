# Sensitive Artifact Policy

## SOC 2 report

SOC 2 reports require NDA approval before sharing.

## Penetration test report

Penetration test reports require NDA approval and Security review before sharing.

## Architecture diagram

Detailed architecture diagrams require NDA approval and Security review before sharing.

## Approved wording

Sensitive security artifacts may be made available to qualified customers under NDA after Security and Legal review.
